Cannelonni

Cannelloni are some big pasta filled!

Cannelloni are large, tubular pasta typically stuffed with a savory filling like ricotta, spinach, or meat, then baked with sauce and cheese for a hearty, comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Dried cannelloni tubes
  • Ricotta cheese (250g)
  • Cooked spinach, chopped (200g)
  • Ground meat (beef, pork, or both, 250g) - optional
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (50g)
  • Egg (1, beaten)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Pepper and salt as you wish

Steps to follow

  1. Prepare Filling: Mix ricotta, spinach, ground meat (optional), Parmesan, egg, garlic, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
  2. Make Sauce: Sauté onion in olive oil, add tomato sauce, and simmer with seasoning.
  3. Stuff Cannelloni: Fill tubes or fresh pasta sheets with the filling.
  4. Assemble: Spread sauce in a baking dish, add stuffed cannelloni, top with more sauce and cheese.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 mins, then uncover for 10 mins to brown.
